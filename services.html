Our Services

See detailed descriptions of our services below!

Day Care

Our daycare offers a structured, engaging environment where every day is filled with fun and enrichment. Your dogs will enjoy a balanced routine of supervised play sessions, social interaction, nature walks, and mentally stimulating games tailored to their age and energy level. They're never pushed beyond their comfort, and We schedule regular rest breaks to prevent overstimulation and provide cosy spaces for naptime. Fresh water is always available, and we're happy to serve owner-provided meals or treats throughout the day.

Training

Our training program focuses on essential skills that build a strong foundation for well-mannered dogs. We cover commands such as sit, stay, come, and down. We also cover lead walking, impulse control, and polite greetings. Training sessions are kept short, positive, and rewarding to keep dogs motivated. Our approach is tailoured to each dog's individual pace and personality, using proven, force-free methods to encourage lasting learning.
