We Go the Extra Mile

We're Insured

We are fully insured by Protectivity to provide professional care and training, giving you peace of mind. Our coverage includes both daycare services and training, ensuring that every dog is protected while receiving the attention, structure, and support they need.

We're Certified

Our daycare is proudly certified to meet high standards of canine care and training. All staff members are trained in Pet First Aid and have completed courses with the Institute of Modern Dog Trainers (IMDT), ensuring your dog is in capable hands.

Guaranteed Satisfaction

We proudly stand by our promise of guaranteed satisfaction. From the moment your dog joins us, their happiness, safety, and progress are our top priorities.
